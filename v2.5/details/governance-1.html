Update 4 May 18, 2026

On May 18, 2026, the state cabinet officially approved the formation of a high-level inquiry commission to investigate institutional corruption, bribery, cut money, and financial mismanagement across central and state social and construction projects.

Led by retired Calcutta High Court Justice Biswajit Basu and member-secretary K. Jayaraman (ADG, North Bengal), the commission will commence operations on June 1, 2026.
