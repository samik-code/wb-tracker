Update 3 May 21, 2026

The Transport Department issued Notification No. 1723-WT/TPT/18011/30/2026 on May 21, 2026, officially enacting free bus travel for all women in West Bengal starting June 1, 2026. Women can initially travel by showing any valid government photo ID to receive a Zero Value Ticket, but must eventually transition to a QR-coded Smart Card.

📌 Note: Private Bus Dominance

The free travel scheme is strictly limited to state-run buses (WBTC, SBSTC, NBSTC) and excludes private buses. Historical transport data indicates private buses operate about 80% of routes in West Bengal, significantly limiting the policy's real-world coverage.

Update 2 May 18, 2026

On May 18, 2026, the state cabinet gave policy approval to allow free travel for women in government-run buses, effective June 1, 2026. The state government announced that the scheme will initially utilize the existing fleet and infrastructure of state-run transport systems.

While no immediate expansion of the fleet is planned, the administration indicated that a proposal for new electric buses would be introduced in the upcoming June budget session.
